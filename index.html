How it works

AGRISUP is an e-commerce platform that connects farmers directly with consumers, eliminating middlemen to reduce costs. By offering affordable, fresh produce and reliable, timely delivery, AGRISUP helps combat food inflation and supports Nigeria’s agricultural sector with a 4% platform fee.

The Problem

Rising food prices in Nigeria are making fresh produce expensive and harder to access. Middlemen inflate costs, and inefficient logistics cause delays and waste.

The AGRISUP Solution

AGRISUP connects farmers directly to consumers, cutting out middlemen and reducing costs. Our logistics partners ensure timely delivery, and a 4% platform fee makes it affordable for all.

Market Opportunity

Agriculture makes up 25% of Nigeria’s GDP, and 65% of Nigerians prefer online shopping. With food inflation at 24.8%, AGRISUP meets the demand for fresh, affordable produce.

Competitive Advantage & Financial Outlook.

AGRISUP’s direct connections lower prices, and reliable logistics ensure fresh produce. We project ₦3 million in Year 1 revenue, with steady growth, and an initial startup cost of ₦1,000,000.
